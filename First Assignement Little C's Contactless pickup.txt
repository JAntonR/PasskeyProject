User request:
Little C's wants a contactless pickup option for picking up orders.

It is similar to the Amazon lockers, where you come and no one is physically there, but you give some biometric or a passkey that allows you to pick up the order.

Dennis says they have a device that allows the detection of users depending on the distance of the device. 

They want to do it incorporating this distance detection Bluetooth device and using passkeys or some biometric to assure arrival

Example:
Ben orders pizza from his house. Ben leaves his house, and Little C starts making the pizza. Little C finishes the pizza and places it in the heated locker. Ben arrives at the locker, and the locker UI/manager greets him. Ben gives his credentials to the machine, and the locker opens up and gives him his pizza. Ben takes the pizza, closes the locker, and eats it on his way home.
